Chrono Terms of Service
Last Updated: Subject to Change

This is essentially a list of rules for our platform. We reserve the right to update this at any time without any notice, so please check here frequently in case the terms are updated. For privacy information, click here.

A violation of any of these rules could result in a temporary or permanent ban. New accounts will usually be permanently banned on their first infraction, while older accounts may be given temporary bans depending on severity.

Text Guidelines

These apply to all text inputs on the site, including forum posts and in-game chat.

  • Do not discriminate or use the N word. Use of this word will result in termination regardless of context. Swearing is allowed, but excessive profanity is not.
  • Do not reveal personal information about yourself or others. Age verification requests by staff are exempt.
  • Do not start drama or public conflicts. Do not create posts exposing other users. Contact a moderator if you have concerns.
  • Do not excessively advertise external websites or platforms. Casual mentions are allowed, spam is not.
Image Guidelines

These guidelines apply to all images uploaded to the platform.

  • Do not upload gore or NSFW content under any circumstances.
  • Do not upload pornographic content. This will result in immediate termination.
Other Notes
  • Owning alternate accounts will result in termination.
  • You are responsible for your account’s security. Password recovery is handled case-by-case.
  • Do not use the advertising system for unrelated URLs. Only Chrono-related or relevant community links are permitted.
